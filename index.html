Squirrelll.ing

Community-based micro-fintech for round-ups, daily saving habits, and smart money goals.

Squirrelll.ing helps people save and invest in tiny daily steps. The platform is designed around spare-change round-ups, budgeting tools, goal-based saving, and a community Daily Pool mechanic that makes consistent saving easier to keep up with.

What Squirrelll.ing offers

  • Round-up savings that turn everyday purchases into small automated contributions.
  • A Daily Pool concept for community-based saving motivation and gamified progress.
  • Budget and round-up calculators that help users estimate savings potential.
  • A pre-launch email waitlist for early access to the mobile app.
